Yoga AI: Bio-Feedback Meditation System

Advanced Computer Vision & Real-Time Physiology Analysis

1. Project Overview & Working

Yoga AI is a cutting-edge, interactive wellness application that bridges the gap between ancient yogic wisdom and modern artificial intelligence. It functions as a personal meditation assistant that monitors both physical posture and physiological state in real-time.

How It Works:

  • Computer Vision (The "Eyes"): Uses a webcam to track 33+ body landmarks and 21 hand keypoints. It detects specific yoga mudras (hand gestures) and analyzes spinal alignment/posture.
  • Bio-Sensing (The "Heart"): Connects to an Arduino-based Pulse Sensor to capture real-time heart rate (BPM) and Heart Rate Variability (HRV).
  • AI Synthesis (The "Brain"): Fuses visual and biological data to calculate "Prana" (Energy), "Focus" (Concentration), and "Stress" levels.
  • Interactive Feedback: The UI responds dynamically—auras change color, graphs visualize energy flow, and audio cues guide the user based on their actual state.

2. Why This Project is Unique & Special

Most fitness apps are passive trackers. Yoga AI is an active, closed-loop system.

  • Holistic Integration: It's one of the few projects that combines Computer Vision (Pose) with IoT/Hardware (Heart Rate) in a single Python application.
  • "Chakra" Visualization: Instead of boring charts, it translates dry data into engaging visuals like "Chakra Energy" and "Aura Colors," making bio-feedback intuitive and spiritual.
  • Smart Mudra Recognition: It doesn't just see hands; it recognizes specific gestures (Gyan, Prana, Anjali) and triggers specific app modes (e.g., "Prayer Mode" on Namaste).
  • Privacy First: All processing happens locally on the device. No video is sent to the cloud.
